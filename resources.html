Resources

Everything your gamer heart needs

Partnerships

From the get-go we worked closely with partners essential to the gaming experience: gaming studios, equipment manufacturers and game magazines. With our partnerships, we can offer you access to top-of-the-line gaming equipment at our locations, give you insights into the newest developments and launch unique event collaborations!

Equipment

Not only offer we access to different kinds of gaming equipment, we also offer events where you can test out new hardware and get unique insights from brand ambassadors. Our community is also thriving with recommendations, reviews and tips to make sure you get the best out of every piece of your equipment.

Walkthroughs

Stuck on your fourteenth try of defeating that blasted Elden Ring boss? Want to make sure you find every possible collectible in your favourite game? You can find all that and more in walkthroughs, easily accessible in our community. Either created by fellow players or sponsored by one of our partners, they will not let you down!

PC Builds

Still stuck on that computer from 2016 but unsure how to update your hardware? Or are you looking to dip your toe into the unknown waters of building your first computer yourself?
In collaboration with our partners, we offer guided and tested information about the best components and builds for all your different needs. During events, you can learn how to build from experts hands-on and test out hardware before making the decision to buy it. And of course the community has your back with lots of personal reviews and preferred set-ups for your inspiration!
